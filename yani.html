Yani


Yani is one of our four rescue llamas .
He came to us with Eric at the end of 2009 after having been kept as “ornaments” in a field for around 6 years, after which it appears the owners sold the property, moved away and just left Eric and Yani in the field.




We are not sure of Yani's exact age but believe he was born sometime around 2003.
He was left unhandled for around 6 years and also seems to have suffered from some inappropriate handling methods before he came to us which has left him very wary of being handled, particularly of having a halter put on him.
We are very slowly gaining his confidence so we are hoping that one day he will have the confidence to be able to join the other llamas on our walks.
