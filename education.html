PEAK HILL LLAMAS



Educational Visits

Peak Hill Llamas welcomes visits from schools, scout and guide groups and other youth organisations.

For a small charge per child we will provide an enjoyable and educational session with the llamas in the llama paddock, followed by a short walk. Prices vary depending on numbers - please contact us for further details.

Vranch House Visit

Vranch House is a centre for the treatment of outpatients with physical difficulties, a provider of various therapies throughout Devon and an independent Day School in Exeter for children with significant physical difficulties.

Peak Hill Llamas visited Vranch House in Exeter with llamas Ollie & Golly.
